About Us

What is Simplelaw?
Simplelaw is a legal database which aims to create a more informed public by organizing laws into clear, navigable, and comprehensible categories. Laws are sorted based on topic and demographic in order to make it easy for any user to learn about the laws which directly affect their lives. Conventionally, individuals acquire legal information either through biased media sources or through non user-friendly government websites. Simplelaw seeks to give individuals the power to easily and independently find and read the laws which affect their lives, rather than relying on third party interpretation.The website is currently in beta, with recent laws from Connecticut available. In order to access laws, either click on Connecticut on the front page map, or go to the "Categories" tab and choose a category.

Who are we?
