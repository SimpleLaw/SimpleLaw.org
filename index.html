SimpleLaw: Your laws, made easy

SimpleLaw strives to give you easy, unbiased access to the laws that affect you.

Currently Under Construction

SimpleLaw is currently in alpha, but at this point the site is mostly operational. Head over to SimpleLaw.org to see what great features to expect. Please note that there are not many laws available right now, and most that are available are from Connecticut.

SimpleLaw only supports laws and bills passed in the United States of America, but may support other countries in the future. If you have any ideas or recommendations regarding current or future features, please feel free to email us at simplelawteam@gmail.com. Also, please support our efforts by starring and forking the project on our GitHub Page!
